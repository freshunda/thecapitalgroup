Meet Our LEADERSHIP TEAM

Fernando Lassiter

Founder

Fernando Lassiter grew up in North Carolina. He is the oldest of three siblings. He attended North Carolina Central University for three years where he studied Accounting. He then decided to pursue small business ownership. He also spent over fifteen years in the retail sales industry selling appliances and electronics. He spent three years in the pharmaceutical manufacturing industry. He was also involved in the direct sales industry where he was introduced to the astounding benefits of Personal Development. Realizing that traditional employment could make you a decent living but rarely a six-figure income, he always kept his business options open.

Fernando has always had a passion for working with and helping others. He has always had a Problem Solvers’ mentality.

In 2013, he was presented with a unique opportunity to work with Mr. Sean Simmons and his Insurance Agency, Agent Success Management in Alabama. After three years of management training in the Agent Success Management system, he established The Capital Group Insurance Agency. The Capital Group Insurance Agency is the second largest and second highest producing agency under the ASM umbrella.

Fernando is extremely excited about the future of The Capital Group Insurance Agency. Having earned a six-figure income in his second year with ASM, he’s excited about working directly with the up and coming six-figure earners associated within The Capital Group team. The Capital Group is dedicated to taking care of its Final Expense clients and providing others with the opportunity to grow Personally and Financially through this Great Opportunity. The Best Is Yet to Come!!!!!!

Donaldo Harris

Managing General Agent

Donaldo M. Harris, Sr. is a husband and a father of five beautiful children. Donaldo was born and raised in Virginia. He is the youngest of eight children. Donaldo graduated with his Bachelor of Science in Mathematics and has a Master of Science in Educational Leadership. Donaldo has spent the last twelve years working in the field of education as a School Administrator.

Donaldo has always had a strong connection to people and a love for sales, but needed to find a way to be able to bring the two things he loves together. He knew that working for someone else wasn't the only option and that there had to be a better way to reach his goals. In June of 2017, Donaldo took a leap of faith and walked away from his career in education to pursue his passion for helping people. Donaldo went on to join The Capital Group, which is a part of Lincoln Heritage Life Insurance. He then became the founder of D.R.I.V.E.N (Developing Relationships, Influencing Visionaries, Empowering Neighborhoods) Insurance Group. The mission of D.R.I.V.E.N Insurance Group is to help individuals that are truly seeking change in their lives. D.R.I.V.E.N is changing the faces of many individuals and neighborhoods. Dreams Will Become Reality.

Donaldo M. Harris Sr.

  • Mike Hayes

    Managing General Agent

    Michael Hayes joined Lincoln Heritage Life Insurance Company in July 2012, as an Independent Sales Agent. In September 2013, he was promoted into the role of Managing General Agent (MGA). However, his biggest accomplishment is being a proud husband and father of four (3 daughters and 1 son).
