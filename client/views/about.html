ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Lincoln Heritage Insurance Company was founded in 1963 by Jack London. His
mission was to raise the bar fro prompt, friendly, and accurate service in the life
insurance industry.

Since 2007, the company has grown more than 10% per year.

Today, Lincoln Heritage is the number one Final Expense Company operating in 49
states with over 690,000 policies in force.

It's also an Accredited Better Business Bureau, with an A.M. Best rating of A-
(Excellence).

As a family-owned company, Lincoln Heritage provides the old-fashioned service you
deserve.
